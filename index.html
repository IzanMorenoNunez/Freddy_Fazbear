About Us

Fazbear Entertainment was founded in 1987 by two lifelong friends, Henry Emily and William Afton, in the heart of Salt Lake City, Utah. What began as a small pizzeria with a dream to bring joy to families quickly grew into a beloved chain of animatronic restaurants. Henry, a talented engineer, designed the first animatronic characters—Freddy Fazbear, Bonnie, Chica, and Foxy—to entertain children while they enjoyed their meals. William, a savvy businessman, focused on creating a welcoming atmosphere that would make every visit unforgettable. Despite early challenges, including a fire in 1992 that temporarily closed their flagship location, their dedication to innovation and family entertainment helped Fazbear Entertainment thrive.Our mission is to create magical experiences where families can laugh, eat, and make memories together, surrounded by the wonder of our animatronic friends. We envision a world where every child’s imagination is sparked, and every meal is a celebration of joy and creativity. Today, Fazbear Entertainment continues to grow, with locations across the country, staying true to our roots of combining great food with one-of-a-kind entertainment.
